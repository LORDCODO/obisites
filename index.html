Giant Killing

"Giant Killing" is a Japanese anime and manga series. It's a sports anime that focuses on soccer (football). The story revolves around East Tokyo United (ETU), a struggling soccer team, and its efforts to regain its former glory. The team has been going through a tough period, but with the help of the coach Tatsumi Takeshi and his unique coaching methods, they start to turn things around.
